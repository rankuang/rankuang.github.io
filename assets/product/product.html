z.k. flora

the product

ZK FLORA makes a chair more interesting in form and more playful to assemble.

Specific function is not given to this piece of furniture. It can be a chair as well as a wall decoration.

assembly process

The assembling process of ZK FLORA is simple and fun. An easy cross turns the flat units into a flower-shaped chair back, from which ZK FLORA gets its elegant name.

With three 'clicks', three chair legs can be easily attached to or detached from the chair back. A chair for the family diner or simply a wall decoration, it's up to you.
