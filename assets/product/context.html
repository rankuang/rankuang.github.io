context

expensive house rate

Nowadays, the population living in big cities in China is constantly growing. Take Shanghai for example, residential population has grown from 13.8m to 24.2m in 20 years. Among the huge residential population, a large proportion of it is migrant population. In fact, the migrant population rate has grown from 6% to 40% in the same time period. Migrant job seekers, college students make up most of them. These citizen groups are not able to purchase huge houses in a short time, so they chose to rent small apartment instead.

small apartment vs. artistic home

Though living in a small apartment, the desire of living in an artistic home has not been demolished. Yet still small apartment has its limitations. Limited size and lack of storage space are the biggest problems.

Therefore, to solve this problem with an artistic design is the challenge we are facing.
